Modeling and Forecasting Atmospheric Concentrations of NOx using Satellite data

C.S. Track: AI

Mentor: Ramesh Natarajan

Members: Ori Bach, Aaron Poulad, Elchonon Stein

The problem we are interested in is monitoring, modeling and forecasting atmospheric NOx concentrations, which have strong localized effects in space and time, and are primarily due to anthropogenic activity involving fossil fuel combustion due to transportation, power generation and industrial activity. As such, NOx concentrations are important for pollution studies (e.g. smog), but also as a proxy for the more hard-to-measure CO2 emissions which is the primary GHG responsible for long-term global warming.

Torah Navigator

C.S. Track: AI

Mentor: Moshe Radinksy

Members: Alex Neugroschl, Jeremy Wizenfeld, Moshe Wieder

Torah Navigator is a recommendation engine for YU Torah, utilizing machine learning algorithms to deliver personalized lecture suggestions to users. By analyzing user preferences and interaction history, Torah Navigator enhances the learning experience by providing tailored content that matches individual interests and study patterns.

C.S. Track: DIS

Mentors: Steven Mauceri, Shreya Nagpar

Members: Yosef Bejman, Jacob Borenstein, Edmond Shrem, Yaakov Seif, Gabriel Loloey

JobScout

C.S. Track: DIS

Mentors: David Mandelbaum, Sohan Gharpure

Members: Nessim Betesh, Sam Stolarov, Adir Koenig, Ilan Wurmann, Owen Caplan

JobScout is a web application that simplifies job searching by querying multiple job listing sites and saving user-defined searches. Utilizing a distributed work scheduler, it regularly updates the job listings database and notifies users of new opportunities. The application also features a user-friendly web interface for manual queries and real-time results.
